Why choose Floris Cattery?

We are a professional cattery that specializes in breeding fluffy teddy bear-like British Short/Longhair cats. We are a small family-owned cattery, meaning that our cats are a part of the family. They receive lots of love and training from us so that they can fit right into your family. We have strong ethical standards to ensure you are satisfied with your kittens. We only breed healthy cats free of FIV and FeLV (genetically tested) with a pedigree registered with WCF and TICA. This means that our cats are 100% purebred with an excellent pedigree and bloodline. You can trust us to find the cat of your dreams!
